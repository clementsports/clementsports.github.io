Equipment

The boys will need to have basketball shoes for practices and games so that they can get the best traction on the court.

From ASA guidelines: "All players must wear gym appropriate shoes for basketball."

Basketballs - each boy should have their own 27.5" size ball for practice and before games.

Boys will need lots of water. Ensure that they have an adequate water bottle for practices and games. Since they will be running around a lot, I don't want them to run out.
